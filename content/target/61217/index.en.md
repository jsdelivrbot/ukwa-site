# Shirenewton & Mynyddbach Fields Association Limited (SAMFAL)

The Shirenewton & Mynyddbach Fields Association Limited (SAMFAL) was established in 2006 to protect and enhance the biodiversity and special ecological value of the 'Green Wedge' between the two villages. Shirenewton & Mynyddbach are two villages on the outskirts of Chepstow.
