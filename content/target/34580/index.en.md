# Bragdy'r Beirdd

Noson y Beirdd (Night for the Bards) is a Welsh language literary night held at Canton Sports Bar, Cardiff. It was established by Welsh poets Catrin Dafydd, Osian Rhys Jones a Rhys Iorwerth.
