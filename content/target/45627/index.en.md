# Hackney Flashers: Work of a Women's Collective 1974-1980

The Hackney Flashers collective was set up in 1974 and created influential agitprop material in the 1970s and 1980s. The women in the group, described themselves as broadly socialist-feminist and most of them engaged in the creative media as photographers, cartoonists and writers.
