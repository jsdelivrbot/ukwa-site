# UK Parliament: Scottish Affairs Committee publications

<p>Reports, responses and written and oral evidence published by the Scottish Affairs Committee. The House of Commons Select Committee is appointed to examine the administration, expenditure and policy of the Scotland Office, including relations with the Scottish parliament. Publications deal with the Scottish Referendum and implementation of UK policy in Scotland.</p>
