# Bank of England: Financial Policy Committee statement from its meeting - 12 March 2018

The Financial Policy Committee (FPC) reviewed the outlook for UK financial stability, risks to UK financial stability from Brexit, and risks from crypto-assets. The FPC also finalised the main elements of the design of the 2018 stress test of major UK banks.
