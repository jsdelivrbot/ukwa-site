# Janice Atkinson (@Janice4Brexit) on Twitter (Independent MEP for the South East of England)

Janice Atkinson MEP was elected to the European Parliament in 2014 and represents the south east of England. She was a member of the UK Independence Party at that time but subsequently left and sat as an independent. In the 2010 General Election fought a marginal constituency for the Conservative Party.
