# Social Market Foundation

The Social Market Foundation (SMF) is a non-partisan think tank. It believes that fair markets, complemented by open public services, increase prosperity and help people to live well. It conducts research and runs events looking at a wide range of economic and social policy areas, focusing on economic prosperity, public services and consumer markets. The SMF is resolutely independent, and the range of backgrounds and opinions among its staff, trustees and advisory board reflects this.
