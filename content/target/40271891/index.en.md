# Damian Collins (Conservative Politician)

Damian Collins stood as Conservative candidate for Folkestone and Hythe at the 2015 General Election. He was first elected to serve as MP for Folkestone and Hythe in 2010 and re-elected in 2015. He stood again for re-election in 2017 and was successful.
