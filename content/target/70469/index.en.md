# BBC News: Brexit: No-deal 'risks break-up of UK' - Herman Van Rompuy

Herman Van Rompuy told the Observer leaving the EU without a deal posed an "existential threat" to the UK. The former Belgian prime minister added that he believed no-deal comments from government ministers were part of "operation fear", intended to scare the EU into compromising on a deal.
