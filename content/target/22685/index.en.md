# David Tredinnick (Conservative Politician)

David Tredinnick stood as Conservative candidate for Bosworth at the 2015 General Election. He was first elected to serve as MP for Bosworth in 1987 and re-elected in 2015. He stood for re-election in 2017 and was successful.
