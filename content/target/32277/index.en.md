# The Great War Society.

The Great War Society is a non-profit making organisation based in the U.K. Founded in 1984, we provide an opportunity for practical research into the uniforms, weaponry, equipment, training and everyday tasks of the Great War soldier, of both the Allied forces and the Central Powers.
