# Nawr yr Arwr | Now The Hero is

Nawr yr Arwr / Now the Hero is 'an immersive theatrical experience by
Marc Rees held in September, 2018 across the city of Swansea. It will be
an extraordinary journey through three intertwining narratives of war;
from Celtic history, the First World War, and today's conflicts. It will
involve a requim by Icelandic composer Jóhann Jóhansson in collaboration
with Welsh composer Owen Morgan Roberts with a libretto by Owen Sheers
sung by Polyphony, Stephen Layton’s world-famous choir.
