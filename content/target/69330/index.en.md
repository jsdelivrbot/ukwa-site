# Dynevor Revisited

This website is a focal point for all past Dynevor Schools and is for former pupils and staff from Dynevor Secondary Grammar School, Dynevor Senior Comprehensive School and Llwyn-y-Bryn Girls' School, which amalgamated to become the new Dynevor Comprehensive School in 1978.
