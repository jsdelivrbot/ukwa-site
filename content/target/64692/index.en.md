# BBC News: Brexit: Morgan calls for 'rational debate' on UK's customs plans

Former education secretary Nicky Morgan said speculation that Theresa May could face a challenge if MPs ended up backing some form of post-Brexit customs union was "deeply unhelpful". MPs were to debate a backbench motion on Thursday April 26th 2018 calling for the UK to retain this type of arrangement after Brexit.
