# SOCIAM: The Theory and Practice of Social Machines

SOCIAM is an EPSRC funded project, examining social machines: how to make them effective and sustainable, and to understand their development and the skills required to produce them. Social Machines are defined as web-based tools that provide data administration, enabling human users to engage creatively.
