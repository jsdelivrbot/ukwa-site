<p>VivaCity2020 is a university-led research consortium, comprised of Lancaster University, the University of Salford, University College London, London Metropolitan University, and the University of Sheffield, along with over 100 partner organisations.<br />
In 2003, VivaCity2020 received funds from the Engineering and Physical Sciences Research Council (EPSRC) to undertake a five-year programme of research into the design and delivery of sustainable urban environments (SUE). The tools and resources developed address issues of crime, economic vitality, social exclusion, the environment and the quality of urban life. The project has produced numerous publications.</p>
