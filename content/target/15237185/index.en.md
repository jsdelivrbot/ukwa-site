# Proceedings of the Old Bailey - London's Central Criminal Court, 1674-1913

The Proceedings of the Old Bailey project is a collaboration between the Universities of Hertfordshire and Sheffield and the Open University,  funded by the Arts and Humanities Research Council and the Big Lottery Fund. The website contains searchable texts containing 197,745 criminal trials held at London's central criminal court.
