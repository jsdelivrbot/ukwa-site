# Rowecord Engineering

Rowecord Engineering was one of the UK's Principal Structural Steel Fabricators and offered design, fabrication and construction of Steel framed Structures & Steel Bridges. It was based in Newport  and went into liquidation in 2013 and brought by AIC Steel Limited.
