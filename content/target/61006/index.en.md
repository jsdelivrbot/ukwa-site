# French Studies Library Group (FSLG)

The aim of the French Studies Library Group (FSLG), which was founded in 1999, is to act as a focus for librarians and others concerned with the provision of library resources and services in French studies in the United Kingdom. This site was closed in 2012 and is now available at https://frenchstudieslibrarygroup.wordpress.com/
