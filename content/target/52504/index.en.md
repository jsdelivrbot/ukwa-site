Tim Farron stood as Liberal Democrats candidate for Westmorland and Lonsdale at the 2017 General Election and was elected. He was first elected to serve as MP for Westmorland and Lonsdale in 2005 and re-elected in 2010 and 2015.He was elected as Party leader in 2015 and resigned on 14 June 2017, remaining as leader until a successor is elected. He is a strong supporter of remaining in the EU.
