# National Library of Wales: Dyfrlliwiau John Ingleby | John Ingleby Watercolours

John Ingleby was a native of Halkin in Flintshire. He was a topographical artist who specialised in producing small watercolour views which were ideal as illustrations for the antiquarian studies of Thomas Pennant (1726-1798). Many of his works were digitised by the National Library of Wales.
