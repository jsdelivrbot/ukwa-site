# Steam Car Club of Great Britain, The

<p>The Steam Car Club of Great Britain was formed in recognition of increasing interest in steam cars and the need for a standalone club dedicated to that need. It has produced a magazine 'The Steam Car'. The membership spans many countries including Australia, New Zealand, America and Europe.</p>
