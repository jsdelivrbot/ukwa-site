# Two Worlds of Charlie F, The

The Two Worlds of Charlie F is a play by Owen Sheers drawn from the personal experience of wounded, injured and sick Service personnel. It premiered at the Theatre Royal Haymarket, London, in January 2012 and toured nationally and then was revived for an international tour in 2014.
