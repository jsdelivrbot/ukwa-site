# Spirit Health Education Network (SHEN)

Founded in 2008, Spirit Health Education Network is a charitable organisation based in Forres, Scotland, that draws on a network of international experts and ancient learnings. Their vision is to raise global awareness through traditional means by promoting: spirituality; environmental sustainability; health and well-being; cultural education.
