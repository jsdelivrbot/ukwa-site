# Shuttle - Blackburn with Darwen Council News, The

The Shuttle is the civic magazine of Blackburn with Darwen. It is written and published by the Council and delivered to 60,000 homes. It gives you information about the work the Council and its partners do.

This website complements the print version of the Shuttle by bringing you all the latest news from Blackburn with Darwen Council.
