# Department for Social Development Northern Ireland

The Department for Social Development was established as part of the Northern Ireland Executive in December 1999. It has strategic responsibility for urban regeneration, community and voluntary sector development, social legislation, housing, social security benefits, pensions and child support. Around 7,000 people are employed by the Department.
