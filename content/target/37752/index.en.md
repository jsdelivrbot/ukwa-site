# UK Vision Strategy

The UK Vision Strategy, whose aims are supported by UK governments, is implemented through a strong alliance of statutory, health and social care bodies, voluntary organisations, eye health professionals and individuals. To lead the implementation of the UK Vision Strategy across the UK, a Leadership Group meets throughout the year to review progress and plan collaborative work.
