# Spirit of the Rainbow Heron

An inspiring website which showcases an ongoing series of creative activities carried out to support mental wellbeing, in commemoration of a special person who suffered mental health problems and passed away too early, set up as a tribute by her friends, to ensure her spirit lives on.
