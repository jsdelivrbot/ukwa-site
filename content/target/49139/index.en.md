Lynne's Organic Farm is an organic smallholding of just over 35 acres in the High Weald Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty near Eridge in East Sussex. The farm is fully organic, certified by the Soil Association since 2007. They produce organic pork, eggs and fruit and vegetables, all available in their small Farm Shop. 
 
One of their main aims is to farm in a sustainable and environmentally sound way.
