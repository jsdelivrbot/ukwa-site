# TwoRains Blog

TwoRains Blog 

A project using the Indus Civilisation to ask the question “Does climate change really cause collapse?” The ERC-funded TwoRains project is based at the Department of Archaeology, University of Cambridge and is being conducted in collaboration with the Department of AIHC and Archaeology at Banares Hindu University.
