# Confederation of British Industry (CBI): Business and union leaders' joint appeal for Brexit progress

Business and union leaders called on the UK government and the EU to inject pace and urgency in the negotiations, bringing about measurable progress, in particular a backstop arrangement to avoid a hard border in Ireland in advance of the June 2018 European Council meeting.
