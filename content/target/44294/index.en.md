# David Davis (Conservative Politician)

Davis Davis stood as Conservative candidate for Haltemprice & Howden at the 2017 General Election and was re-elected. He was first elected to serve as the MP for Haltemprice & Howden in 1987 and has held the seat ever since. He is secretary of State for Exiting the European Union.
